About Sahu Capital

S
Shubham Sahu
Independent Investment Analyst · Mumbai
LinkedIn Twitter — @hubformoney Substack Instagram

I spent 6 years studying equity markets before placing a single real trade. What started as curiosity became a deep interest in understanding how capital flows, how businesses create value, and how markets price assets — often irrationally in the short term, but with remarkable consistency over time.

In June 2025, I publicly called Sterlite Technologies at ₹80. It hit ₹440. In May 2025, I called Netweb Technologies at ₹1,630. It hit ₹4,479. These were documented research calls — made public before the moves, with clear reasoning and defined risk levels.

Sahu Capital is my platform for independent investment research — free from institutional bias, free from sell-side incentives, and free from the noise that dominates financial media.
